# **Placement LLM – Final Question Taxonomy**

---

## **YEAR 1 (Sem 1–2)**

### **A. CGPA**

### **Awareness & Importance**

- Is CGPA important from first year?
- Do companies consider first-year CGPA?
- How early should I start worrying about CGPA?
- Does first-year CGPA matter for internships?
- Is consistency in CGPA important?

### **Benchmarks & Targets**

- What is a good CGPA in first year?
- Is 7 CGPA bad?
- Is 8+ CGPA realistic?
- What CGPA should I target to stay safe?

### **Recovery & Flexibility**

- If CGPA is low in first year, can I recover?
- Will companies forgive low first-year CGPA?
- How much does one bad semester affect placements?
- Is it okay if CGPA fluctuates early?

### **Strategy & Habits**

- Should I prioritize CGPA over extracurriculars?
- What habits help maintain CGPA?
- How do toppers maintain high CGPA?
- What mistakes should I avoid regarding CGPA?

---

### **B. Skillset**

### **Exploration & Discovery**

- What <skillset> should I start learning?
- How do I choose my first <skillset>?
- How do I know if <skillset> suits me?
- Is it okay to explore multiple <skillsets>?

### **Early Learning Strategy**

- Is it too early to start working on <skillset>?
- How much time should I spend weekly?
- Should I follow a roadmap or explore freely?
- What beginner mistakes should I avoid?

### **Foundations & Habits**

- What habits help improve <skillset> early?
- How important is consistency?
- Should I document my learning?
- What non-technical skills support <skillset>?

### **Long-term Value**

- How does first-year <skillset> help later?
- Are certifications useful in first year?
- When should I become serious for placements?
